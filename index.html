Welcome to The Simpsons Pairs

Aim of the game

The aim of the game is to find all matching Simpsons character pairs before the time runs out. You are only allowed to turn 2 cards over at the same time, so you will need to remember where the characters are once they have been revealed. For every matching pair that you turn over you will receive 50 points and a time bonus will added to the timer. The amount of time added will depend on the game difficulty chosen.

Game difficultyTime bonus
Easy (12 cards)5 seconds
Medium (20 cards)4 seconds
Hard (28 cards)3 seconds

Mistake penalties

If you fail to turn over a matching pair after the matching character card to the first card turned has already been revealed, you will receive time and score deductions. Again the level of game difficulty chosen will determine the amount of deductions received.

Game difficultyTime PenaltyScore Penalty
Easy-2 seconds-5 points
Medium-5 seconds-10 points
Hard-10 seconds-20 points
